T/t - toggle trail 

camera movement:
w,a,s,d,z,x
w, s - camera zoom in and out
a, d - camera Left and Right
z, x - camera moving linearly up and down

Camera rotation:
Up arrow, down arrow, left arrow, right arrow, c, v
Up arrow - camera looking up
Down arrow - camera looking down
Left arrow - camera looking left
Right arrow - camera looking right 
c, v -camera rotating about z-axis

Scroll up- zoom in
Scroll down- zoom out
Left mouse - drag

M/m - enabling mouse following movement (only in free rotation)

F/f - toggle full screen

Backspace - Clear trail

Escape - Pause Menu
